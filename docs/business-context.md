# Business Context

## Problem Statement

Maritime logistics operations involve complex coordination across terminals, vessels, and regions. Delays, inefficiencies, and bottlenecks in cargo movement can lead to increased operational costs and reduced service quality.

However, these issues are often difficult to detect early due to fragmented data and lack of centralized visibility.

---

## Objective

This project provides a centralized analytics solution to monitor terminal performance, identify bottlenecks, and evaluate cargo movement efficiency using data-driven insights.

---

## Target Users

- **PMO (Project Management Office)**  
  For tracking performance KPIs, monitoring project impact, and supporting governance reporting.

- **Operations Teams**  
  To identify underperforming terminals, detect delays, and optimize resource allocation.

- **Logistics & Supply Chain Analysts**  
  For analyzing trends, comparing vessel categories, and supporting decision-making.

- **Management / Stakeholders**  
  For high-level visibility into operational efficiency and performance risks.

---

## Business Value

- Enables proactive identification of bottlenecks and inefficiencies  
- Supports data-driven decision-making in logistics operations  
- Improves transparency across terminals and regions  
- Provides a structured, executive-ready reporting solution  

---

## Use Case

A logistics manager can use this dashboard to:
- Monitor real-time performance across terminals  
- Identify terminals with high bottleneck rates  
- Analyze performance trends by vessel category and time  
- Take corrective actions to improve operational efficiency
